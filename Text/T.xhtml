NOTE: In Korean, when two words combine to create a new compound word, sometimes its pronunciation changes. When this happens, ㅅ (시옷) is used as the final consonant of the first word in order to bridge the two words. It’s called 사이시옷 saisiot, because it’s inserted between (사이) the two separate words. Pay attention to the pronunciation of the words below.
